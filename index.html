Who The hell Am I?

My name is Ali Nagi and I am a proud Yemeni American. I grew up in the amazing Washington Heights. If you want to know what that was like, just listen to 'In The Heights' by Lin Manuel Miranda.

I'm a technologist exploring the tech world. I've done everything between between UX prototyping, Front End Development and writing up servers in Python and Golang.

Seeing everything that's being developed nowadays motivates and inspires me on a daily basis. I strive to use open-source tools to create stuff and ultimately make the world a better place.

Check out some of my prototypes on my Medium Account (and give me some claps por favor). And check out my code on Git!

In my free time, I enjoy lifting, hiking, biking and both playing and watching basketball. I try to draw as often as possible.
